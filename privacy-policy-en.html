Privacy Policy

App: Find My Car  |  Last updated: July 3, 2026

In short: Find My Car does not send any of your data to the internet or to any server. Photos and GPS coordinates you save stay exclusively on your phone until you delete them yourself.

1. What data the app collects

Find My Car accesses the following data solely to perform its core function — helping you find your vehicle after parking:

  • Camera — used to photograph where you parked your vehicle. The photo is stored only locally on your device.
  • Precise GPS location — used to record the coordinates of the parking spot at the moment the photo is taken, and later to calculate direction and distance back to that spot when you request navigation.

2. How data is used and stored

All data (photos and GPS coordinates) is stored exclusively locally, in your phone's internal storage (SharedPreferences and the app's internal file system). The app:

  • does not send data to any external server,
  • has no account, login, or user profile,
  • does not collect data for analytics purposes,
  • does not use cookies or track user behavior.

3. Sharing data with third parties

The app does not share, sell, or transmit your photos or location to any third party, company, or service.

The only exception is when you personally choose to use the "Navigate (Google Maps)" button — in that case, the app passes the saved point's coordinates to the Google Maps app (if installed) via the standard Android inter-app sharing mechanism. This only happens when you explicitly trigger it, and Google Maps' own privacy policy applies to that step, not this one.

4. Permissions requested

  • Camera — to photograph the parking spot.
  • Precise location (GPS) — to record and navigate back to the parking spot.

All permissions are requested only when needed, and you can revoke them at any time in your phone settings (Settings → Apps → Find My Car → Permissions). If you revoke a permission, the related feature will not work until you grant it again.

5. Data deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • you can delete any individual photo/parking entry directly in the app (a "Delete" button next to each item, with confirmation),
  • uninstalling the app deletes all data it has stored.

6. Children's data

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years old.

7. Advertising

The app currently does not display ads. If this changes in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before the change takes effect, along with a clear in-app notice.

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. The date of the last change is listed at the top of the page. Continuing to use the app after changes means you accept the updated version.
